Dear Resident,

As part of the Phase One Evacuation Directive, the Unified States of Redro Wen will initiate a public auction for verified relocation tickets.

Due to the extreme scarcity of space-bound capacity, ticket allocation will be offered through a merit-based, asset-weighted bidding system. Citizens in good standing may participate by submitting verified bids through the Secure Civic Exchange Portal. Bids must include an up-front monetary pledge in Redro Wen Credits (RWC), and may be supplemented by authorized assets such as intellectual property, labor bonds, or family legacy contributions.

Minimum bid requirements will be reviewed and adjusted quarterly in accordance with inflation, fuel availability, and ongoing vessel maintenance overhead. Note: historic indicators suggest initial bids will exceed 400,000 RWC per person.

While all citizens are encouraged to participate, priority access will be granted to high-tier contributors, resource magnates, essential government personnel, and legacy contract holders. Individuals unable to meet financial minimums may apply for reduced-fee review via the Secondary Allocation Lottery.

This initiative is not only designed to ensure the logistical viability of evacuation efforts, but to serve as a self-sustaining funding mechanism for outbound vessel construction, propulsion reserves, and onboard survival infrastructure. All proceeds will directly support Phase Two expansion efforts and future civilian welfare initiatives.

For comprehensive application guidance, audit protections, or payment certifications, visit your nearest Treasury-Linked Processing Annex or contact your Regional Compliance Officer.
