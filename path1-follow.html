By Lina Veras | Staff Reporter
April 8, 2025 • Mistwood, Northern Territory
Photo taken by a local hiker just minutes before the reported sighting. [1]
Residents near the Mistwood region woke up to eerie silence and reports of an unidentified figure cloaked in fog. Multiple witnesses described seeing a person — or possibly something less human — standing motionless in the center of the trail before vanishing. Local hunter Ray Alvers stated, “It was standing dead still. Tall, covered in some kind of robe, and it shimmered — like heat waves in the air.” While no injuries were reported, authorities have advised the public to avoid Mistwood until further notice. This marks the third sighting this month, following similar events in the neighboring Ridgepine and Hollowend regions. “I’ve never seen anything like this in my 17 years of patrol,” said Forest Warden Talia Murn. “Whatever it is, it doesn’t want to be found — but it does want to be seen.” Experts from the University of Hidden Realms are reportedly analyzing environmental anomalies from the area. Locals are calling the figure “The Watcher,” and theories range from spirit folklore to time travelers. A formal investigation has been launched. Forest Daily will continue to follow the story as it unfolds. [2]
