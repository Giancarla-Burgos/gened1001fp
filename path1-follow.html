Observational Findings from the Northern Forest

Author: Elira Vantrell, PhD
Affiliation: University of Hidden Realms

Abstract: This study outlines firsthand observations following the brief encounter with an unknown humanoid figure deep within the northern mistwood. Unexpected anomalies in time perception and environmental feedback were documented.

1. Introduction

The Mistwood region has long held rumors of ghostly wanderers and interdimensional rifts. Until recently, no empirical observations were recorded. On the 3rd day of Spring’s Shift, a figure was seen appearing and disappearing amidst thick fog, exhibiting behavior inconsistent with known physics.
