Mysterious Stranger Spotted in the Forest

By Forest Daily Staff Writer | April 8, 2025

Early this morning, a traveler reported encountering a hooded figure deep within the northern part of the forest. Witnesses say the stranger appeared suddenly, muttered a strange phrase, and vanished into the mist.

"I was just trying to find my way home," said the traveler, who asked to remain anonymous. "But when I looked up, they were already gone."

The mayor has issued a warning to all residents and travelers to avoid the area until further notice. However, rumors suggest that those brave enough to follow the stranger may uncover the forest's long-lost secrets.
